Lehlohonolo Magoete

Founder & CEO

With a background in Human Resource Development, Lehlohonolo Magoete is an inspiring young black female entrepreneur who founded Ziyakha Engineering Pty Ltd, a pioneering safety solutions provider dedicated to enhancing workplace and community safety.

She envisions an Africa in which every workplace and community is equipped with the tools and knowledge necessary to ensure safety for all. Her mission is to provide innovative safety solutions that empower individuals and organizations to prioritize safety and prevent accidents through education, technology, and proactive strategies.
